About this page

I developed a fully responsive landing page, maintaining a high fidelity to the original design. Additionally, I added a footer with contact information and other relevant details. To enhance the user experience, I incorporated dynamic animations, buttons with three states, and a functional form.

The technologies applied include Bootstrap, CSS, JavaScript, and SASS. Using SASS allowed me to maintain clean and organized code, making it easier to understand and maintain. I also utilized specific color codes to ensure visual consistency throughout the page.

I paid close attention to the naming of elements and the structure of the code to ensure functionality and accurately replicate the original design.
